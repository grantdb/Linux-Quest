# Privacy Policy for Linux Quest

Last updated: April 14, 2026

## 1. Information Collection
`Linux Quest` is a webview-based simulation game on Reddit. 

- **Data we access:** The application accesses your Reddit username and public profile information to manage game scores.
- **Data we collect:** We collect and store your **Reddit username** and your **highest game score** in our global leaderboard.
- **Data Storage:** Scores are stored securely in Reddit's `redis` database. No other personal data, browsing history, or private communications are accessed or stored.

## 2. Storage and Security
All game data and leaderboard entries are hosted within Reddit's official developer platform (Devvit). We do not use third-party databases or external trackers.

## 3. Third-Party Access
Your information is never shared with, sold to, or used by third parties. Leadersboard data is public within the subreddit where the game is played.

## 4. Consent
By playing `Linux Quest` and submitting your score, you consent to your username and score being displayed on the public leaderboard.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via Reddit.
